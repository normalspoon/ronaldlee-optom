About

A bit about me:

I graduated from the University of New South Wales with Honours in Optometry and spent time practising in regional NSW before returning to Sydney, where I practised in Top Ryde City. I now practise across various locations in Sydney.

I am experienced in all aspects of optometry and enjoy finding a vision solution for my patients. I have special interests and experience in children’s vision, myopia management, Orthokeratology (OrthoK) contact lenses and advanced contact lens fitting for keratoconus. I have additional qualifications in Advanced contact lenses (ACO) as well as the UNSW - Dry eye disease course. I also am a supervisor for final year optometry students from UNSW and the University of Auckland and enjoy teaching the next generation of optometrists.

Special interests

Skills and experience:

Children's Vision & Myopia Management

I have conducted comprehensive paediatric assessments and work with evidence-based myopia control, including atropine, OrthoK, MiSight and DIMS lenses.

Orthokeratology (OrthoK)

I have experience with corneal topography and fitting different orthokeratology lens designs. I am a member of the Australian College of Optometry (ACO) and hold the Advanced Certificate in Advanced Contact Lenses.

Advanced Contact Lenses

I have experience fitting specialty lenses for keratoconus and complex corneas, using both hard and soft options and tailoring each fit to get the best vision we can.

Dry Eye Management

I have extensive experience diagnosing and managing dry eye disease, and I completed additional training through the UNSW Dry Eye Disease and Management course.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

I have experience across full-scope optometry: ocular disease detection, visual fields, OCT imaging, and co-management of glaucoma and post-surgical care.

Student Supervision

I supervise final-year optometry students from UNSW and the University of Auckland and enjoy mentoring the next generation of optometrists in clinic.
